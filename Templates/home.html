Welcome to Skillshare!

Your new community platform for sharing skills and learning from others. Whether you're a master guitarist willing to teach or someone who wants to learn coding, this is the place to connect, learn, and grow.

Offer the skills you have, earn points, and spend them to learn new skills from others in the community.

How Our Point System Works

Total points are a combination of Base Points (Difficulty) and a Rarity Bonus.

1. Base Points (By Difficulty)

Difficulty Points Earned (Teaching) Points Spent (Learning)
Easy +5 -5
Moderate +10 -10
Difficult +20 -20

2. Rarity (Bonus / Penalty)

Rarity Earn Bonus Spend Extra Cost
Common +0 -0
Uncommon +3 -3
Rare +5 -5
Example: A 'Difficult' skill (Base: +20) that is also 'Rare' (Bonus: +5) will earn you 25 points for teaching it.
